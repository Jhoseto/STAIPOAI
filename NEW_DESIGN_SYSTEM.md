# New Design System (Cyber-Glass Style)

## Colors
- Background: #0a0a14 (Deep Dark)
- Neon Green: #22c55e (oklch(0.7 0.25 150))
- Neon Blue/Purple: #6877ff (oklch(0.65 0.2 255))
- Border Glow: 1px solid with box-shadow

## Components
- Glass Panels: backdrop-blur-xl, semi-transparent background
- Glowing Borders: neon accents on hover or active state
- Typography: Inter/Sans-serif, bold headers, light body text
